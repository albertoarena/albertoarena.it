I'm a senior software engineer with over 10 years building production-grade web applications and e-commerce platforms, and I take on a limited number of freelance and consulting engagements alongside my full-time work.

My foundation is **PHP and Laravel**: I architect maintainable, high-performance backends and have delivered complex **Shopify** and headless commerce solutions for real-world businesses. In recent years I've been deliberately moving toward AI-enhanced engineering, integrating Large Language Models into SaaS platforms and designing AI-assisted workflows that act as a genuine productivity multiplier, not a buzzword.

## What I can help with

- **Laravel architecture & audits**: reviewing an existing codebase, untangling technical debt, or designing a new application from scratch
- **Shopify & headless commerce**: custom storefronts, app development, and integrations for stores that have outgrown off-the-shelf themes
- **LLM integration**: adding AI-assisted features to an existing product, from prototyping to production
- **Backend API design & system integration**: connecting services, designing APIs that will still make sense in two years
- **AI-assisted developer tooling**: workflows and automation that improve velocity without sacrificing maintainability

## How I work

I'm available remotely and open to freelance projects, long-term collaborations, and short consulting engagements (architecture reviews, code audits, or a second opinion on a technical decision). I care most about projects where technical rigor meets genuine problem-solving, where a good decision today prevents an expensive one tomorrow.

If that sounds like a fit, get in touch.
