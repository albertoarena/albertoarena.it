This privacy policy explains how this website collects and processes data when you visit [albertoarena.it](https://albertoarena.it).

## Data controller

Alberto Arena  
Website: [https://albertoarena.it](https://albertoarena.it)

## What data is collected

Only analytics data is collected, and only with your explicit consent. This includes:

- Pages visited and navigation paths
- Referring website or source
- Browser type and version
- Device type and operating system
- Approximate geographic location derived from IP address (country/city level)

No advertising data, personal profiles, or cross-site tracking data are collected.

## How it is collected

Analytics data is collected via **Google Analytics 4 (GA4)**, loaded through **Google Tag Manager (GTM)**. No tracking scripts are loaded until you explicitly accept analytics cookies via the cookie banner.

## Legal basis

Data is processed on the basis of your **consent** (GDPR Art. 6(1)(a)). You can withdraw consent at any time by clicking "Decline" in the cookie banner, which appears when you clear your browser cookies and revisit the site.

## Data retention

Google Analytics retains data for **14 months** by default. You can request deletion of your data through your Google account or by contacting the data controller.

## Third parties

Analytics data is processed by **Google LLC** acting as a data processor. Google may transfer data outside the EU/EEA. For details, see [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

No other third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services are used.

## Cookies used

| Cookie | Purpose | Expiry |
|--------|---------|--------|
| `_ga` | Distinguishes unique users | 2 years |
| `_ga_XXXXXXXXXX` | Stores session state for GA4 | 2 years |
| `cookieConsent` | Stores your consent choice (first-party, never shared) | Persistent |

## Your rights

Under GDPR you have the right to:

- **Access** the data held about you
- **Rectification** of inaccurate data
- **Erasure** of your data
- **Withdraw consent** at any time — clear your browser cookies to reset the cookie banner, then click "Decline"
- **Lodge a complaint** with your local data protection authority
